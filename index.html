This course offers a comprehensive and rigorous introduction to computer vision — the study of how computers can be made to gain understanding from digital images or videos. Students will explore both classical and modern methods, from low-level image processing through high-level recognition and generation. Lectures cover the mathematical foundations, practical algorithms, and deep learning techniques currently used in vision systems.
